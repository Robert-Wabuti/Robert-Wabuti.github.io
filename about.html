About

Conveniently located within town just a few meters from the busy central business district Royal Delight Hotel and Apartments is the perfect choice for both business and leisure traveler. A quiet serene and relaxing environment, the hotel offers 24 vibrant modern rooms with free wireless Internet Access, On-site restaurant, Terrace restaurant, a free parking and a brilliant team at your service. We are well equipped to accommodate and host guests visiting for vacation, leisure corporate and private meetings.

Location & Convenience

Whether you’re traveling for business or pleasure, you’ll find this hotel’s location in section 9 of Thika town. The quiet and serene environment provides a suitable space for in-house office work with provision of free Wi-Fi and a business center. The proximity to Thika business district is only 10 minutes drive, 30minutes drive to Nairobi city and 45 minutes to the Airport.
